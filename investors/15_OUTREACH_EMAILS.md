## Usage Notes

- **Find contacts via LinkedIn** using titles like: VP Partnerships, Head of Product, Director of Integrations, Chief Compliance Officer, VP Business Development
- **Don't use info@ or sales@ as the first contact.** Find a named person.
- **Wait 5 business days** before following up once. If no response to the follow-up, move on.
- **The GitHub link and demo link are the only assets.** No PDF attachments, no pitch deck, no slide links in the first email.
- **If they reply and ask for more:** send the [Partner Outreach Strategy doc](./14_PARTNER_OUTREACH.md) as a reference frame, and the [Friday Positioning Memo](./11_FRIDAY_POSITIONING_MEMO.md) as context. Do not forward the financial projections or token terms docs until they ask.
