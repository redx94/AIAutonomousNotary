## 2. Unit Economics & Revenue Streams

The protocol operates directly on-chain, eliminating corporate overhead while capturing algorithmic fees across **three distinct revenue streams**:

### Stream A: Primary Issuance (The Protocol Fee)
- **Traditional Cost**: $15 per document
- **AI Notary Cost**: $8 per document
- **Fee Distribution**:
  - $3 to the Notary Professional (if multi-party) or AI Node Validator network.
  - $2 to cover gas and decentralized storage (IPFS/Arweave).
  - **$3 Net Protocol Revenue** (Treasury / Token Buy-and-Burn).

### Stream B: Secondary Market Trading (The AMM Fee)
Once a document (e.g., Real Estate Deed) is fractionalized into ERC-20 shares in our Vault, it trades on our decentralized marketplace.
- **Trading Fee**: 0.5% protocol fee on all secondary trading volume.
- *Example*: A $1M commercial real estate deed sees $5M in annual trading volume. Protocol captures **$25,000** in passive revenue from a single asset.

### Stream C: Document Lending / Escrow Origination
- **Origination Fee**: 1.0% fee on all loans issued against notarized document collateral.
